Data Analysis




This tool analyzes phosphoproteomic data. To see a relative estimate of the activity of the kinases known to phosphorylate the substrates in your dataset, please upload a file containing 7 columns (Substrate, control_mean, inhibitor_mean, fold change, p-value, control_CV, and inhibitor_CV. This analysis can only handle one inhibitor at a time, files must have csv or tsv extensions.
