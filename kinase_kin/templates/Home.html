Kinase Kinpendium

Research with Ease

Kinase Kinpendium is a web application that allowes researches to access background information on all known Human Kinases, their phosphorylation sites & the inhibitors that work on them. Users can also upload quantitative phosphoproteomics data to be analysed. Please see below for an idea of what functionality Kinase Kinpendium provides


Search Kinases

Click Kinases tab on the top of any page to search Kinases by thier symbols or Uniprot Accession Numbers & get background information on their gene symbols, the families they belong to, cellular location & further annotation.

Search Kinase Inhibitors

Click on the Inhibitor tab on the top of any page to search Inhibnitors and find out about; their respective ChEMBL ID's, chemical structure, which kinases they are known to inhibit & other information

Search Subsrates

Click on the Substrates tab on the top of any page to search kinase substrates and find out; which Kinase phosphorylates them, browse genomic location, neighbouring sequences, modified residues & other information

Upload Data

Click on the Data Analysis tab on the top of any page to upload quantitative phosphoproteomics data (be sure to follow upload instructions) to visualise the corresponding relative Kinase activity of your sample.
